Welcome to the calibration wizard

The grid below represents the space that the Leap Motion can detect.

If your right hand is in view of the Leap, it will appear as a black dot inside the grid.

When the calibration direction changes, move your hand to the position that accompanies that movement, for example, if the direction is forward, you would move your hand forward.

Once you have reached your desired position, make a fist for 3 seconds and your choice will be confirmed.
